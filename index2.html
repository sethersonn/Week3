Dark Souls II Boss Souls

In Dark Souls II, there are multiple way to utilize boss souls. You can consume them for various amounts of souls depending on which is consumed, or trade it for a weapon or spell with Straid of Olaphis or Weaponsmith Ornifex. Listed below are the souls dropped by bosses, and what you get from either trading or consuming it.
